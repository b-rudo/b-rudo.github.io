    Honorable Mention for Virtual Reality Demo Award
    Issued by Dr. Sabarish Babu, Clemson University (Dec 2018)

Users were able to enter a doctor's office, lay down in a realistic representation of a MRI machine, and receive a virtual scan. Simulation created as a final project for the course CPSC 4110 - Virtual Reality Systems.
