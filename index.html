Working Fluency Seal of Bilteracy (English & Spanish)
Issued by Global Seal of Biliteracy (May 2020)
Certifies that the individual can "...handle both routine and unfamiliar situations in both formal and informal settings. This person is able to adjust responses in the target language to respond to a unique circumstance or question. They can make specific recommendations or suggestions based on client feedback, give detailed explanations and precise descriptions of services, terms, procedures and policies, resolve problems, or justify a company policy or procedure. Although their communication may include grammar or pronunciation errors, they have enough accuracy to be understood any speaker of the language."

Honorable Mention for Virtual Reality Demo Award
Issued by Dr. Sabarish Babu, Clemson University (Dec 2018)
Created a VR simulation with three teammates to alleviate anxiety and claustrophobia for those preparing to undergo a MRI scan utilizing graded exposure therapy. Users were able to enter a doctor's office, lay down in a realistic representation of a MRI machine, and receive a virtual scan. Simulation created as a final project for the course CPSC 4110 - Virtual Reality Systems.
